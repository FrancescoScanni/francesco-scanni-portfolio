Job related AI Bias - ImGen

I built a website to show how image-generating AIs can reflect bias. I tested different prompts and compared results, noticing patterns in gender, race, and stereotypes. The site includes examples and short reflections on what I found.

Education

Linear Regression - AI & ML

I followed a guided project to learn linear regression with Python and scikit-learn, aiming to predict a child’s height based on the parent’s height. I learned how to prepare data, train a model, and check its accuracy.
